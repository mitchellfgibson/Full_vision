Long-Term Vision

Decentralized Investor Hubs.
Connected By Shared Infrastructure.

Investor communities will become one of the most powerful forces in finance over the next decade.

BTHI aims to help build highly engaged local investor communities — connected through shared infrastructure:

  • Transparency
  • Education
  • Technology
  • Media
  • Relationships
  • Collective intelligence
Local communities. Shared infrastructure. Aligned incentives.
  • Every hub runs independently — local leadership, local deal flow, local relationships.
  • One shared playbook — Ctable, education, and tooling power every community underneath.
  • Capital and intelligence flow freely between hubs, compounding with every new city.
  • Investors win when companies win — incentives aligned by design, not by hope.

Live events Founder dinners Community diligence Local ambassadors Investor meetups Online collaboration Education Deal sourcing
